    A timer, not a medical test

    HearWise records time and the volume label you choose. It does not measure sound pressure, diagnose hearing loss, or replace advice from a hearing professional. If you notice ringing, pain, muffled hearing, or sudden changes, stop listening and seek appropriate care.
